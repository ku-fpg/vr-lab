Download this one to save the world.

So this time I started you out with a plane and a couple boxes, and even included some textures to make them look pretty.

The reason for the plane is that we're now in the magic world where physics exists, and without ground, we'd just fall forever.

For funzies, push the sandy box off the edge and watch it fall and maybe jump after it. Reload the page and repeat at your leisure.

Seriously, I spent like 30 straight minutes trying to bounce on the falling box. It's like impossible to get 2 consecutive bounces on it, even though you fall faster than it. I challenge you to try it though.

To explain a little about the physics: static-bodies will never move. Kinetic-bodies can be moved by the camera and other forces. And Kinematic bodies are things that the player can move and can apply force to kinetic-bodies, so effectively only the camera ever has this.

Assignment time!

I've given you the tools, so here is your mission. Make a basic platformer.

Start on the platform given, and place static-body objects out in open space, jump to them to then jump to a higher plane.

So for example, you start out on your plane. You jump up about 3 boxes and then jump onto another plane higher up.

Tip though: Planes are only visible from one side, you can either use this to your advantage or use a box or something else in its place.

Tip 2: You might want to consider changing your jump distance to get "better ups", as some may say.

Feel free to be as creative as you want! You can add lights, shadows, just about anything. And there are a whole lot of other objects out there, so you don't have to only use boxes and planes.
